# VitaPlate Smart Notifications - User Guide

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Access Notification Settings
1. Click your profile avatar in the top-right corner
2. Go to **Settings** → **Notifications** tab
3. You'll see all notification options grouped by category

### Step 2: Customize Your Preferences

#### 🌍 Timezone & Quiet Hours
- Select your timezone (so reminders are at the right time)
- Toggle "Quiet Hours" to prevent notifications between 10 PM - 7 AM
- ✅ Save when done

#### 🍴 Meal Logging Reminders
- **Enable Meal Logging Reminders** - Get reminders for breakfast, lunch, dinner
- Set custom times for each meal reminder
  - 🥞 Breakfast: Default 9:30 AM (if no breakfast logged by then)
  - 🍽️ Lunch: Default 2:00 PM (if no lunch logged by then)
  - 🍷 Dinner: Default 8:00 PM (if no dinner logged by then)

#### 🔥 Streak Alerts
- **Enable Streak Risk Alerts** - Reminds you not to break a streak
- Only triggers if you have a 3+ day streak and haven't logged meals yet
- Default time: 9:00 PM
- Helps you stay motivated and maintain momentum

#### 📅 Weekly Reminders
- **Weekly Plan Reminder** - Sunday 7 PM: "Your plan for next week is ready"
- **Weekly Health Digest** - Monday 8 AM: Email summary of your nutrition
- **Goal Check-In** - Weekly nudge to review your progress

#### 💊 Other Reminders
- **Supplement Reminders** - Morning reminders for your supplements (optional)
- **Lab Result Reminder** - Reminder when it's been 90 days since your last lab upload

### Step 3: Save Settings
- Click **Save Notification Settings** button at the bottom
- You'll see a success message

## Using the Notification Center

### View Notifications
- Click the **🔔 bell icon** in the top navigation
- See all your notifications in the dropdown
- **Blue dot** = unread notification

### Mark as Read
- Click a notification to read it
- It automatically marks as read and may navigate you to relevant page
- Use **Mark all read** to clear unread count

## Tips & Best Practices

### ✅ Do's
- ✅ Adjust reminder times to match your actual meal times
- ✅ Keep quiet hours enabled to avoid sleep disruption
- ✅ Enable streak alerts if you use streaks for motivation
- ✅ Review weekly digest on Monday mornings
- ✅ Check lab reminder when you're due for new results

### ❌ Don'ts
- ❌ Don't disable meal reminders if you struggle with consistency
- ❌ Don't set breakfast reminder at 6 AM if you sleep until 9 AM
- ❌ Don't ignore streak alerts if you care about your streak
- ❌ Don't skip lab uploads for more than 90 days

## Troubleshooting

### "I'm not seeing meal reminders"
1. Check if "Enable Meal Logging Reminders" is toggled on
2. Make sure you haven't already logged that meal today
3. Check if you're in quiet hours (10 PM - 7 AM)
4. Verify your timezone is correct

### "Reminders are at the wrong time"
1. Go to Notifications settings
2. Check your timezone is correct
3. Adjust reminder times if needed
4. Save settings

### "I'm getting too many notifications"
1. Go to Notifications tab
2. Toggle off notification types you don't want
3. Extend quiet hours if needed
4. Save settings

### "I want to pause notifications temporarily"
1. Go to Notifications tab
2. Extend quiet hours (e.g., 6 AM - 11 PM)
3. Or disable specific notification types
4. Save settings

## Notification Delivery

### In-App Notifications
- Appear in the bell icon
- Show unread count badge
- Click to read and navigate
- Available 24/7

### Quiet Hours
- Active from 10 PM to 7 AM by default
- NO notifications during quiet hours
- You can customize the hours or disable entirely

## FAQ

**Q: Will I get notifications if I'm not on the app?**
A: Currently, notifications are in-app only. They appear in the bell icon when you next open the app. Push notifications are coming soon!

**Q: Can I get SMS or email reminders?**
A: Currently, in-app notifications are available. Weekly digest emails can be enabled for more detailed health summaries.

**Q: What if I already logged a meal before the reminder time?**
A: The system checks if a meal is logged for that meal type on that day. If it is, no reminder is sent.

**Q: Can I customize which days I get reminders?**
A: Currently reminders are daily. You can disable them entirely or adjust times. Future versions may support "weekdays only" or custom schedules.

**Q: What if my timezone isn't listed?**
A: Let us know! We can add more timezones. For now, select the closest timezone to your location.

**Q: How do I know if quiet hours are on?**
A: Go to Notifications settings. If "Quiet Hours" toggle is on, they're active from 10 PM - 7 AM (or your custom times).
